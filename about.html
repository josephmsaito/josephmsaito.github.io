About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Psychology at the University of Toronto working with Keisuke Fukuda Lab. I earned my B.A. working with Benjamin J. Levy at the University of San Francisco (2016) & completed a brief stint as a lab manager with Nathan S. Rose at the University of Notre Dame (2017–2019).

My work uses psychophysical and neurophysiological methods (EEG) to characterize voluntary control over the cascade of visual memory processing from encoding to maintenance to retrieval.

Outside the lab, I am probably watching (or yelling at) my favorite Bay Area sports team, hitting the gym, or trying the newest trendy restaurant in my area.
